Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Pizza is another classic Italian dish the features a crust with cheese, tomato and pepperoni on top.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Tomato Paste
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Make the pizza sauce.
  3. Make the pizza dough.
  4. Add the sauce, cheese and pepperoni to the dough.
  5. Cook in the oven.
