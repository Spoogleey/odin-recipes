Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is the classic Italian dish consisting of meat, pasta and sauce. It is a quick and easy dish that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
